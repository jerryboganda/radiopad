# Code of Conduct

RadioPad adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We — contributors, maintainers, and clinical reviewers — pledge to make participation in RadioPad a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Standards

Examples of behaviour that contribute to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism.
- Focusing on what is best for the radiologists, patients, and clinical safety of the product.

Unacceptable behaviour:

- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, personal or political attacks.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing private health information (PHI), even synthetic data styled to resemble a real patient.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional clinical setting.

## Enforcement

Report incidents to **conduct@radiopad.example** (placeholder — replace with the project mailbox before public launch). Reports are handled confidentially. Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as decided by the project leadership.

## Scope

This Code applies in all project spaces — repositories, issue trackers, mailing lists, chat channels, and conferences — and when an individual is officially representing the project.
