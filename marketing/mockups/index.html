Questions radiology teams ask first.

Does the AI ever sign reports?
No. RadioPad drafts and validates, but a radiologist reviews and signs every report. There is no setting that lets the model sign or send on its own.
Where is patient data stored?
You can run RadioPad on-premise or fully local, so protected health information never has to leave your environment. Cloud deployment is optional, not required.
Which modalities does it support?
RadioPad works across CT, MRI, X-ray, ultrasound, and more. Reporting is driven by your templates and rulebooks, so it adapts to how your department reads.
Can we use our own templates and rulebooks?
Yes. The rulebook engine encodes your department’s standards, and reports are generated into your existing templates rather than a fixed house style.
Does it integrate with our PACS or RIS?
RadioPad exports FHIR DiagnosticReport alongside PDF, DOCX, and plain text, so it fits into existing PACS and RIS workflows. Integration scope is confirmed during onboarding.
